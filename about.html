The Story

Heguricho Techonologies is a Nara registered Technical Services company providing technical marketing services in proven technologies of manufacturing right from Design, ,high end manufacturing to mass production. We thank you for visiting our website. Partnering with K-Tech Co., Ltd., Nara, Japan since 2001, we have been together for the past twenty years for supply of high class machinery to Indian, European, South East Asian and American markets.

Our customers include Maruti Suzuki Ltd, Bajaj Auto Ltd, TVS Motor Co Ltd for high end Amino Presses for sheet metal forming, Toyoda Gosei Pvt Ltd, PMP Auto Steering Ltd, Sundaram Auto Components Ltd, Funskool Ltd, Minda Ltd. for used injection molding machines, Attitude Plastics Pvt Ltd for high speed die milling and Acuratech Pvt. Ltd., Virgo Engineers Ltd, Lunarmech Maschinen Fabrik Ltd, Sharpline Automation Pvt Ltd for used machine tools including high end 5 axes machine tools to name a few. We are leaders in supply of high end used machines. Retrofit specialist including Sharpline Automation, Confidence Automation Pvt. Ltd., prefer us as suppliers of high end machines. Sheet metal machinery users like Nash Industries buy from us.

We are at present approved sellers of industrial assets owned by Japanese Banks and Leasing companies across the globe.

With India now foraying boldly in the storm of this pandemic we are ideally placed in India and Japan to address your machinery needs in the fields of Semi-conductor, Electronics, Optics including LED light source, Display related manufacturing including hand held smart phone parts manufacture.
